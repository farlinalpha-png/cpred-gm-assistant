CP:RED PLAYER COMPANION
=======================
Two ways to use it:

1. STANDALONE (offline): open player.html in any browser (double-click).
   Characters save in the browser. Works with no internet or GM.

2. VIA GM HOST: when the GM clicks "Host Session" in the GM Assistant,
   they get an address like 192.168.1.42:8045. On the same network,
   either open http://THAT-ADDRESS in your browser, or use the standalone
   player.html and enter the address in the GM Sync tab.

Sync: while connected, your character changes push to the GM every few
seconds and GM-side edits flow back. Offline changes sync on reconnect.
"Upload to GM" sends the open character immediately.
